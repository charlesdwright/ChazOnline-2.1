WHO IS CHAZ?

Indeed. But...

Programming, aka "Software Development," or, most recently, "Software Engineering," which really means "implementing someone else's software." Think "indentured servitude." To whom, you ask? Big Tech. You're not its only slaves; we are, too.


I owned a business and so I needed to crunch numbers. A natural aversion to repetitive tasks drove me to automate them. An aversion to risk meant that I could earn a better risk-weighted return as a programmer than I did as a trader.

I avoided a career in tech for the same reason I avoided Van Halen records: I was put off by its constituency. Later in life I discovered that Eddie Van Halen [RIP] was a virtuoso, an innovator, an artist. I also learned that repetitive tasks can be rather meditative.

Enough about me.
